# Welcome to ForLarge

ForLarge is a digital marketplace where creators sell beats, sound kits, music, templates, and other digital products directly to buyers using cryptocurrency.

## Why ForLarge?

- **Direct payments** — Buyers pay creators directly via blockchain. No intermediaries holding your money.
- **Multi-chain support** — Accept payments on Solana and Injective (Cosmos), with more chains coming soon.
- **Instant settlement** — Funds arrive in your wallet the moment a buyer completes a purchase.
- **Low fees** — Just 5% platform fee. No hidden charges, no monthly subscriptions.
- **Secure downloads** — Time-limited, token-gated download links protect your content.

## How It Works

### For Creators

1. **Sign up** with an invite code or Google account
2. **Set up your profile** — username, bio, avatar
3. **Connect your wallet** — Solana (Phantom/Solflare) or Injective (Keplr/Leap)
4. **Upload products** — beats, sound kits, templates, anything digital
5. **Share your store** — your unique URL at `forlarge.app/store/yourusername`
6. **Get paid** — USDC arrives directly in your wallet when someone buys

### For Buyers

1. **Browse** products on the Explore page or visit a creator's store
2. **Connect your wallet** — Phantom, Solflare, Keplr, or Leap
3. **Pay with USDC** — on Solana or Injective
4. **Download** — receive a secure, time-limited download link instantly
