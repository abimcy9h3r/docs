# Uploading Products

ForLarge supports any digital product — beats, sound kits, templates, presets, sample packs, and more.

## Creating a New Product

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Products**
2. Click **New Product**
3. Fill in the product form

### Product Details

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Title** | Product name shown to buyers | ✅ |
| **Description** | Detailed description of the product | ✅ |
| **Price** | Price in USDC | ✅ |
| **Category** | Product category (beat, sound kit, etc.) | ✅ |
| **Product File** | The downloadable file (up to 100MB) | ✅ |
| **Cover Image** | Thumbnail image for the listing | ✅ |
| **Preview Audio** | Short audio preview for audio products | Optional |

### Supported File Types

- **Audio**: MP3, WAV, FLAC, AIFF
- **Archives**: ZIP, RAR (for bundles/kits)
- **Documents**: PDF
- **Images**: PNG, JPG, PSD, AI

<Warning>
  Maximum file size is **100MB**. For larger bundles, compress your files into a ZIP archive.
</Warning>

## Product Pricing

Prices are set in **USDC** (USD Coin), a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. This means $10 USDC = $10 USD.

- Minimum price: $0.50 USDC
- Buyers pay in USDC on their selected blockchain
- You receive 95% of the sale price
- 5% goes to the ForLarge platform fee

## Editing Products

To edit an existing product:

1. Go to **Dashboard → Products**
2. Click the **Edit** button on the product card
3. Update any fields
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Product Visibility

Products can be in two states:

- **Published** — visible on your store and in Explore
- **Unpublished** — hidden from buyers, only visible in your dashboard

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write Compelling Descriptions">
    Include genre, mood, BPM, key, and any included stems or variations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use High-Quality Cover Art">
    Your cover image is the first thing buyers see. Make it stand out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Audio Previews">
    For beats and audio products, always include a short preview clip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Price Competitively">
    Check similar products on the platform to find the right price point.
  </Step>
</Steps>
