# Setting Up Your Store

Every creator on ForLarge gets a personal storefront at `forlarge.app/store/yourusername`.

## Creating Your Profile

After signing up, you'll be taken through onboarding to set up your profile.

### Username

Your username determines your store URL. Choose something memorable and professional.

- Must be unique across the platform
- Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only
- Cannot be changed after creation (contact support if needed)

### Bio

A short description that appears on your store page. Tell buyers what you create and why they should follow you.

### Profile Picture

Upload a profile image that represents your brand. Square images (400×400 or larger) work best.

## Your Store Page

Your public store page displays:

- **Profile** — your name, bio, and avatar
- **Products** — all your published listings
- **Share button** — lets visitors share your store

Buyers can browse your products and purchase directly from your store page.

## Dashboard Overview

The creator dashboard at `/dashboard` gives you:

- **Products** — manage listings (create, edit, unpublish)
- **Sales** — view transaction history and revenue
- **Analytics** — track views, purchases, and conversion rates
- **Settings** — update profile information and wallet addresses
