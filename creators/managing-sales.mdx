# Managing Sales

ForLarge gives you full visibility into every transaction, payment, and download.

## Sales Dashboard

Navigate to **Dashboard → Sales** to view your complete transaction history.

Each sale record includes:

- **Buyer email** — who purchased the product
- **Product** — which product was sold
- **Amount** — price in USDC
- **Chain** — which blockchain was used (Solana, Injective)
- **Transaction hash** — on-chain proof of payment
- **Date** — when the purchase occurred
- **Status** — verified, pending, or failed

## Revenue Tracking

Your revenue summary shows:

- **Total revenue** — lifetime earnings across all products
- **This month** — current month's earnings
- **Total sales** — number of completed transactions
- **Average order value** — your average sale price

<Info>
  All revenue figures show the amount you received (after the 5% platform fee is deducted).
</Info>

## Transaction Verification

Every payment on ForLarge is verified on-chain before a download link is issued:

1. Buyer submits a payment transaction
2. ForLarge queries the blockchain to verify:
   - Transaction is confirmed
   - Correct amount was sent
   - Payment was sent to your wallet
   - Platform fee was included
3. Only after verification does the buyer receive a download link

This process is fully automated and typically takes a few seconds.

## Analytics

Navigate to **Dashboard → Analytics** for performance metrics:

- **Product views** — how many times each product was viewed
- **Conversion rate** — views to purchases ratio
- **Top products** — your best-selling items
- **Revenue over time** — track trends in your earnings

## Payouts

ForLarge does **not** hold your funds. Payments go directly to your connected wallet:

- **Solana** — USDC arrives in your Solana wallet
- **Injective** — USDC (Peggy) arrives in your Injective wallet

There are no payout schedules or minimum thresholds. You receive funds the moment a buyer completes a transaction.

## Disputes and Refunds

Since blockchain payments are irreversible, ForLarge does not currently support refunds through the platform. If you want to refund a buyer, you can send USDC directly to their wallet address.
