# Welcome to Forlarge

Forlarge is creator monetization infrastructure 
built natively on stablecoin payment rails —
powered by Injective.

Sell digital products, run paid communities, 
and earn stablecoins — no bank account required, 
no geographic restrictions, 95% of your direct 
revenue yours. Always.

The creator economy generates $250 billion 
annually. The platforms serving it — Stripe, 
Gumroad, Patreon — were built for 46 countries. 
There are 195. Forlarge is built for all of them.

## The Problem We Are Solving

Traditional creator platforms are built on 
financial rails that require bank accounts, 
geographic approval, and currency stability. 
The result is systematic exclusion — a music 
producer with 80,000 followers who cannot 
collect payment from a global audience. A 
designer with a product worth buying and no 
reliable way to get paid for it.

Stablecoins solve this at the infrastructure 
level. A stablecoin transfer works identically 
in Lagos, Jakarta, Berlin, and New York. There 
is no geographic restriction on a smart contract. 
No bank account required. No 7-day payout delay. 
No currency conversion eating into earnings.

Forlarge is the commerce layer built on top 
of that infrastructure — and Injective is the 
chain we are building on first.

## Why Stablecoin Rails

Traditional payment processors hold your 
money, convert your currency, and decide 
which countries are allowed to participate. 
Stablecoin rails do none of that.

- **Non-custodial always** — smart contracts 
  split funds atomically at transaction time. 
  Forlarge never holds your money, not even 
  for a millisecond. Creator funds go directly 
  to creator wallets.

- **Dollar-stable earnings** — stablecoins 
  are pegged to the US dollar. A creator in 
  a high-inflation economy earning stablecoins 
  is not watching their earnings lose value 
  every month. What you earn today is worth 
  the same tomorrow.

- **Instant settlement** — funds arrive in 
  your wallet the moment a transaction 
  confirms. No holding periods, no payout 
  schedules, no waiting for a bank to 
  process a transfer.

- **Global by default** — there is no 
  geographic restriction on a stablecoin 
  transfer. Forlarge works in every country 
  where creators have audiences and products 
  worth selling.

- **Transparent and auditable** — every 
  transaction is recorded on-chain. Every 
  fee is visible. Every split is verifiable 
  independently by anyone, at any time, 
  on the Injective explorer.

## How It Works

### For Creators

1. **Sign up** with Google or connect 
   your wallet
2. **Set up your storefront** — username, 
   bio, avatar — your store is live at 
   `forlarge.app/username` immediately
3. **Upload your product** — any digital 
   file, set your price in stablecoins
4. **Share your link** — send 
   `forlarge.app/username` anywhere your 
   audience already is
5. **Get paid** — stablecoins land in your 
   wallet the moment someone buys. A sale 
   notification email arrives within 
   60 seconds.

### For Buyers

1. **Visit a creator's store** or browse 
   the Explore page
2. **Connect your wallet**
3. **Pay with stablecoins** — sign the 
   transaction in your wallet
4. **Download instantly** — a secure 
   download link appears on screen and 
   arrives in your email within 60 seconds

## Piracy Protection

Every download is served via a signed URL 
that expires in 60 seconds and enforces 
your configured download limits. Files are 
stored in private buckets — there are no 
public URLs for any product file, ever.

Audio files carry a silent forensic 
watermark encoding the buyer's identity 
and transaction details. PDFs carry 
metadata and steganographic watermarks. 
If your content appears somewhere it 
should not, the source is traceable.
