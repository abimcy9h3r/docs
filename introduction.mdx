## Why Forlarge

- **Non-custodial always** — smart contracts 
  split funds atomically at transaction time. 
  Forlarge never holds your money, not even 
  for a second
- **95% to you** — 5% platform fee on 
  direct sales. That is it. No hidden charges, 
  no monthly subscriptions on the free tier
- **Works everywhere** — no bank account, 
  no country restrictions, no approval process. 
  If you have a wallet and a product, 
  you can sell
- **Instant settlement** — USDC arrives in 
  your Solana wallet the moment a transaction 
  confirms on-chain. No 7-day payout delays
- **Piracy protection** — every download 
  is served via a signed URL that expires 
  in 60 seconds and enforces your download 
  limits automatically

## How It Works

### For Creators

1. **Sign up** with Google or connect 
   your Solana wallet
2. **Set up your storefront** — username, 
   bio, avatar — your store is live at 
   `forlarge.app/username` immediately
3. **Upload your product** — any digital 
   file up to 500MB, set your USDC price
4. **Share your link** — send 
   `forlarge.app/username` anywhere your 
   audience is
5. **Get paid** — USDC lands in your 
   wallet the moment someone buys. 
   You receive a sale notification email 
   within 60 seconds

### For Buyers

1. **Visit a creator's store** or browse 
   the Explore page
2. **Connect your Solana wallet** — 
   Phantom, Backpack, or Solflare
3. **Pay with USDC** — sign the transaction 
   in your wallet
4. **Download instantly** — a secure download 
   link appears on screen and arrives in 
   your email within 60 seconds

## Fee Structure

| Sale Type | Platform Fee | You Keep |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Direct sale (you bring the buyer) | 5% | 95% |
| Discovery sale (buyer finds you on Forlarge) | 15% | 85% |
| Subscription billing | 5% | 95% |

Every fee split happens on-chain at 
transaction time. Fully transparent, 
fully auditable on Solscan.
