# Payment Methods

ForLarge accepts cryptocurrency payments on multiple blockchains. All prices are denominated in **USDC** (USD Coin), a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar.

## Supported Chains

### Solana

| | |
|---|---|
| **Currency** | USDC (SPL Token) |
| **Wallets** | Phantom, Solflare |
| **Gas Token** | SOL |
| **Confirmation Time** | ~1 second |
| **Gas Fees** | < $0.01 |

Solana is the recommended chain for most transactions. It offers near-instant confirmations and extremely low gas fees.

### Injective

| | |
|---|---|
| **Currency** | USDC (Peggy) |
| **Wallets** | Keplr, Leap |
| **Gas Token** | INJ |
| **Confirmation Time** | ~2 seconds |
| **Gas Fees** | < $0.01 |

Injective is a Cosmos-based chain popular in the DeFi ecosystem. USDC on Injective uses the Peggy bridge denomination.

## What is USDC?

USDC (USD Coin) is a **stablecoin** — a cryptocurrency pegged to the US dollar. 1 USDC always equals approximately $1 USD.

Using USDC means:
- **No price volatility** — you always know exactly what you're paying
- **Cross-chain availability** — USDC exists on all major blockchains
- **Easy to acquire** — available on every major exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.)

## Getting USDC

### On Solana
1. **From an exchange**: Buy USDC on Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken and withdraw to your Solana wallet
2. **Swap on-chain**: Use Jupiter or Raydium to swap SOL for USDC

### On Injective
1. **From an exchange**: Buy USDC and bridge to Injective
2. **Use Injective Bridge**: Bridge USDC from Ethereum to Injective using the official bridge at [hub.injective.network](https://hub.injective.network)

## Gas Fees

In addition to the product price, you'll need a small amount of the chain's native token to pay transaction fees:

- **Solana**: ~0.000005 SOL (less than $0.01)
- **Injective**: ~0.001 INJ (less than $0.01)

<Info>
  Gas fees on both Solana and Injective are negligible — typically less than a penny per transaction.
</Info>

## Transaction Flow

When you make a purchase:

1. Your wallet creates a transaction sending USDC to:
   - **95%** to the creator's wallet
   - **5%** to the ForLarge platform wallet
2. You sign and broadcast the transaction
3. ForLarge's servers verify the transaction on-chain
4. Once confirmed, you receive your download link

The entire process typically takes under 10 seconds.
