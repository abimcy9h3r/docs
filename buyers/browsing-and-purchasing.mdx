# Browsing & Purchasing

ForLarge makes it easy to discover and purchase digital products from independent creators.

## Finding Products

### Explore Page

Visit [forlarge.app/explore](https://forlarge.app/explore) to browse all available products. You can:

- **Search** by title or keyword
- **Filter** by category (beats, sound kits, templates, etc.)
- **Sort** by newest, price, or popularity

### Creator Stores

Every creator has a public store page at `forlarge.app/store/username`. If you follow a creator on social media, they'll typically share their store link.

## Purchasing a Product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Product">
    Click on any product to view its details, preview audio, and see the price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Buy">
    Hit the **Buy** button to go to the checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Chain">
    Select which blockchain you want to pay on — **Solana** or **Injective**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Connect the corresponding wallet (Phantom/Solflare for Solana, Keplr/Leap for Injective).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide your email to receive the download link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Payment">
    Review the amount and click **Pay**. Approve the transaction in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    After the transaction is verified on-chain, you'll receive a secure download link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Purchase

Once payment is verified:

- You'll be redirected to a **download page** with a secure, time-limited link
- A **confirmation email** will be sent to the email you provided with the download link
- You can download the product immediately

<Warning>
  Download links expire after a limited time for security. Download your product promptly after purchase.
</Warning>

## Purchase History

Currently, purchases are tracked via email receipts. A full purchase history dashboard for buyers is coming soon.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My transaction failed">
    Check that your wallet has enough USDC to cover the purchase price. Also ensure you have a small amount of the native token (SOL for Solana, INJ for Injective) to pay gas fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive a download link">
    Check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find it, contact the creator directly or reach out to ForLarge support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My download link expired">
    Contact the creator to request a new download link. They can verify your purchase on-chain using the transaction hash.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
