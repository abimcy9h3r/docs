# Quick Start

This guide walks you through creating your first product listing on ForLarge.

## Prerequisites

- A Solana or Injective wallet with some funds for testing
- A digital product to sell (audio file, template, etc.)
- An invite code (required during early access)

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit ForLarge">
    Go to [forlarge.app/signup](https://forlarge.app/signup) and create an account using your email or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Invite Code">
    During early access, you'll need an invite code. Enter it on the signup page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Choose a username, add a bio, and optionally upload a profile picture.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

Navigate to your dashboard and click **Connect Wallet** in the top navigation bar.

**Supported wallets:**
- **Solana**: Phantom, Solflare
- **Injective**: Keplr, Leap

<Warning>
  Make sure your wallet is set to the correct network (mainnet for real transactions, devnet/testnet for testing).
</Warning>

## Step 3: Upload Your First Product

1. Go to **Dashboard → Products**
2. Click **New Product**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — the name of your product
   - **Description** — what buyers will get
   - **Price** — in USDC
   - **Category** — beat, sound kit, template, etc.
   - **File** — upload your digital product
   - **Cover image** — a thumbnail for your listing
4. Set your **Solana wallet address** to receive payments
5. Click **Publish**

## Step 4: Share Your Store

Your store is live at `forlarge.app/store/yourusername`. Share this link anywhere — social media, your website, email newsletters.

## Step 5: Get Paid

When a buyer purchases your product:
1. USDC is transferred directly to your wallet (95%)
2. A 5% platform fee is deducted automatically
3. The buyer receives a secure download link via email
4. You can track all sales in your **Dashboard → Sales** page

<Info>
  Payments are verified on-chain by ForLarge's servers before download links are issued. This ensures every transaction is legitimate.
</Info>
