# Fees

ForLarge uses a simple, transparent fee structure with no hidden charges.

## Platform Fee

| | |
|---|---|
| **Fee** | 5% per transaction |
| **Charged to** | Deducted from sale |
| **Creator receives** | 95% of sale price |
| **Platform receives** | 5% of sale price |

<Info>
  There are no monthly subscriptions, listing fees, or withdrawal fees. You only pay when you make a sale.
</Info>

## How Fees Work

When a buyer purchases a product for **$10 USDC**:

1. **$9.50 USDC** goes directly to the creator's wallet
2. **$0.50 USDC** goes to the ForLarge platform wallet
3. Both transfers happen in a **single transaction**

The buyer pays exactly the listed price. The fee split is handled automatically in the smart contract interaction.

## Gas Fees

Blockchain transactions require a small gas fee paid by the **buyer**:

| Chain | Typical Gas Fee |
|-------|----------------|
| **Solana** | < $0.01 (paid in SOL) |
| **Injective** | < $0.01 (paid in INJ) |

Gas fees are paid to the blockchain network, not to ForLarge.

## No Hidden Costs

ForLarge does **not** charge:

- ❌ Monthly subscription fees
- ❌ Account creation fees
- ❌ Product listing fees
- ❌ Withdrawal or payout fees
- ❌ Storage fees for product files
- ❌ Featured listing charges

## Fee Changes

ForLarge's fee structure may be updated as the platform evolves. Any changes will be communicated to all creators with advance notice. Currently active creators will be notified via email before any fee adjustments take effect.
