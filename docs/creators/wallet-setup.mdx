# Wallet Setup

To sell products on ForLarge, you need a cryptocurrency wallet to receive USDC payments.

## Setting Up Phantom (Solana)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Phantom">
    Visit [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) and install the browser extension or mobile app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or Import Wallet">
    Create a new wallet or import an existing one with your seed phrase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Solana">
    Phantom supports multiple chains. Make sure Solana is selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Your Address">
    Click your wallet address to copy it. You'll need this for your product listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Up Keplr (Injective)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Keplr">
    Visit [keplr.app](https://keplr.app) and install the browser extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or Import Wallet">
    Create a new wallet or import with your seed phrase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Injective Network">
    Keplr may prompt you to add the Injective chain. Approve the request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Your Injective Address">
    Your Injective address starts with `inj1...`. Copy it for your listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting Your Wallet on ForLarge

1. Click **Connect Wallet** in the navigation bar
2. Select your wallet from the modal
3. Approve the connection in your wallet extension
4. Your wallet address will appear in the navbar

## Setting Payout Wallets

When creating a product, you specify which wallet should receive payments:

- **Solana wallet** — for buyers paying on Solana
- **Injective wallet** — for buyers paying on Injective

<Warning>
  Double-check your wallet addresses before publishing. Payments sent to the wrong address cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## Security Tips

- **Never share your seed phrase** with anyone, including ForLarge support
- **Use a hardware wallet** for large amounts (Ledger works with both Phantom and Keplr)
- **Keep your browser extension updated** to the latest version
- **Verify transactions** before signing — check the recipient and amount
